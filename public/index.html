📊 Understanding Results

            

              Each validated email receives a score (0-100) and
              an action recommendation:
            

            

              

                
✅ Send Ready (85-100)

                

                  Valid email with excellent authentication (MX +
                  SPF/DKIM/DMARC). Safe to send immediately.
                

              

              

                
⚠️ Need Review (60-84)

                

                  Valid but missing some authentication or has minor concerns.
                  Verify before sending.
                

              

              

                
🚫 Should Suppress (0-59)

                

                  Invalid, disposable, or high-risk. Do not send to avoid
                  bounce/spam issues.
                

              

            

            

              Scoring Breakdown (Max 100 points):

              • Valid syntax: +20 | Domain exists: +10 | MX records: +30

              • SPF: +10 | DKIM: +10 | DMARC: +10 | Known provider: +10
            

          


          

            
⚠️ Risk Flags Explained

            
    
              
  • 
                Disposable: Temporary email service (10-minute
                mail, guerrilla mail). Usually low engagement.
              
    • 
              
  • 
                Role-based: Generic addresses (info@, support@,
                admin@). Often shared inboxes with low deliverability.
              
    • 
              
  • 
                Typo Detected: Common misspellings (gmial.com →
                gmail.com). Suggest correction to user.
              
    • 
              
  • 
                Gibberish: Email appears to be random
                characters with no vowel pattern (e.g., xkjdflwer@domain.com).
                Likely fake or test email.
              
    • 
              
  • 
                Random Pattern: Keyboard patterns (qwerty@),
                repeating characters (aaaa@), or suspicious sequences detected.
              
    • 
              
  • 
                Many Numbers: Unusually high percentage of
                digits (60%+). Often indicates auto-generated or bot emails.
              
    • 
              
  • 
                Confidence Score: Separate from quality score -
                indicates how certain we are about the result (High 90%+, Medium
                70-89%, Low 50-69%).
              
    • 
              
  • 
                ⚠️ Icon: Hover over the warning icon next to
                emails to see specific concerns (provider misconfig, pattern
                issues, etc.).
              
    • 
              
  • 
                No MX Records: Domain cannot receive emails.
                Guaranteed bounce.
              
    • 
              
  • 
                No SPF/DKIM/DMARC: Missing email
                authentication. Higher spam probability.
              
    •

📡 Data Transmission

            
Quick/Standard Validation (Default):

            

              

                1. Browser: Syntax, format, disposable check,
                role-based detection
              

              

                2. Extract Domain: user@example.com
                → example.com only
              

              

                3. Server: DNS lookup (MX, SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
                using domain only
              

              

                4. Response: DNS data returned, email NEVER
                sent to server
              

            

            

              Deep Validation with SMTP (Optional):
            

            

              

                1-4: Same as above (DNS checks)
              

              

                5. SMTP Check: Full email sent to verify
                mailbox exists
              

              

                6. Verification: Server connects to mail
                server, checks if mailbox accepts mail
              

              

                7. Discard: Email immediately discarded from
                memory (NOT logged or stored)
              

            

            

              ✅ DNS checks: Email address NEVER leaves your browser

              ✅ SMTP checks: Email used for verification only, then
              discarded

              ✅ No database, no logging, no data retention
